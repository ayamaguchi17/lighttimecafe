About

OUR COFFEE

Welcome to Light Time Cafe, a place where you can enjoy a perfect cup of coffee, delicious food, and a warm and inviting atmosphere. Our cafe is located in the heart of the city, and we're proud to offer a wide variety of options to our customers. From handcrafted coffee to tasty food, we have something for everyone. At Light Time Cafe, coffee is our passion. We use only the highest-quality beans, sourced from some of the best farms in the world. Our skilled baristas carefully craft each cup to perfection, ensuring that every sip is as delicious as the last. Whether you're in the mood for a classic latte or a bold espresso, we've got you covered. Our coffee menu also features a variety of seasonal and specialty drinks, so there's always something new to try.


In addition to our delicious coffee, we also offer a wide variety of food options to suit any appetite. Our menu features a mix of classic cafe fare, such as sandwiches and pastries, as well as more unique and creative dishes. We use only the freshest and highest-quality ingredients in all of our dishes, so you can trust that whatever you order will be delicious. Our menu is designed to cater to different dietary needs such as gluten free, vegan, and vegetarian options. We also have a wide variety of seating options to suit any need. Whether you're looking for a cozy spot to catch up with a friend, a quiet table to work on your laptop, or a comfortable spot to relax and unwind, we've got you covered. We also have a small garden area, where you can enjoy a relaxing moment with your friends or family.

We pride ourselves on creating a warm and inviting atmosphere at Light Time Cafe. Our decor is modern and minimalist, with a focus on natural light and a neutral color palette. We believe that the atmosphere plays a crucial role in enhancing the overall dining experience, and we have designed our space keeping that in mind. We understand that life can be hectic and time is precious, that's why we are open 7 days a week and we are happy to take your orders for take away if you don't have time to stay and enjoy your meal. We're more than just a cafe - we're a community. We're passionate about providing our customers with the best possible experience, and we're always happy to chat and get to know our regulars. We also organize events and workshops in our cafe, such as poetry readings, book clubs, and cooking classes, to foster a sense of community among our customers.


We also care about the environment, we use biodegradable packaging for take away orders, and we encourage our customers to bring their reusable cups for take away orders. So come on by and see for yourself why Light Time Cafe is quickly becoming the go-to spot for coffee and food in the city. We can't wait to serve you! Whether you're looking for a quick pick-me-up, a place to catch up with friends, or a comfortable spot to work, Light Time Cafe is the perfect choice.
